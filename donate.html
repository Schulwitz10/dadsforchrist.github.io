FAQ

Are gifts tax-deductible?

If you are not yet approved as a 501(c)(3), add: “Gifts are currently not tax-deductible until our 501(c)(3) approval is complete.” Once approved, update this line to reflect tax-deductible status.

Can I cancel a monthly gift?

Yes—every monthly plan includes a “manage subscription” link in your receipt, so you can change or cancel anytime.

Is online giving secure?

Yes. All payments are processed by a PCI-compliant provider over encrypted connections. We never store your card info on our site.

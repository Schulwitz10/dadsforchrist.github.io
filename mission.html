Our Mission

In one sentence: We exist to bring families back to Christ — making Jesus the center of every home.

What we’re after: Dads who lovingly lead and pastor their families, homes that gather for church and love their neighbors, and kids who grow up shaped by the Word of God.

Vision: Where We’re Heading

With God’s help, in the next five years we want to see adoption become easier, fathers grow closer to their children, husbands grow closer to their wives, and fatherless children find support, mentorship, and love in Christ-centered community.

Biblical Foundation

“As for me and my house, we will serve the Lord.”

Joshua 24:15
  • We believe Jesus died and rose again for our sins.
  • We believe the Bible is God’s true Word and the way of life.
  • We call dads to lead with humility, courage, and love.

Key Scriptures to Explore

  • Ephesians 5:25–28 — Husbands, love your wives as Christ loved the church.
  • Ephesians 6:4 — Fathers, bring your children up in the instruction of the Lord.
  • Deuteronomy 6:5–7 — Teach God’s words diligently to your children.
  • 1 Corinthians 16:13–14 — Be watchful, stand firm, be strong, do everything in love.

What We’re Doing Now

  • Podcast: Clear, practical conversations that point dads back to Jesus.
  • Gospel Invitation: Every episode calls people to receive Christ.
  • Free Bibles: We mail KJV Bibles to anyone who asks.

What’s Next

  • Partnerships with churches, ministries, and Bible distributors.
  • More content formats and community support for dads and families.

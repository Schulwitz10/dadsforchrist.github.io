Mission

Dads for Christ exists to call fathers and families back to Jesus, bringing hope and faith into homes, restoring strength to struggling dads, and reviving the American family through the love of our Savior.

Who we are

We’re two brothers—normal, imperfect dads saved by grace—learning to lead our families with Christ at the center. We’re not pastors or teachers; we’re fellow travelers pointing one another back to Jesus.

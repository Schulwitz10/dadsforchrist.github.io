What It Means to Reach One

In Luke 15, Jesus tells the story of a shepherd who had one hundred sheep. When one was lost, the shepherd left the ninety-nine and went after the one until he found it.

“What man of you, having an hundred sheep, if he lose one of them, doth not leave the ninety and nine in the wilderness, and go after that which is lost, until he find it?” — Luke 15:4

That is the heart behind Reach One.

Every soul matters to God. Every person has value. Every father, husband, son, brother, neighbor, and friend is someone Jesus died for.

The Reach One bracelet is not just something to wear. It is a reminder. A reminder to pray for one person. Encourage one person. Invite one person. Share the Gospel with one person.

You do not have to reach the whole world in one day. Start with one.

One conversation can plant a seed. One prayer can change a life. One invitation can open a door. One act of love can point someone back to Christ.

Who is my one?
